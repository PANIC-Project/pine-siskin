Pine Siskin by bensonk

Siskin aims to be a lightweight, linux friendly twitter client, with CLI and GUI interfaces that doesn't suck.

My primary motivation in writing pine siskin is to take advantage of twitter's streaming API, and the ability to provide a more real-time interaction than that provided by polling-based clients.
